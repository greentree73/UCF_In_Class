# Lab: TDD Research Sprint (15 Minutes)

## 🎯 Objective
Research what **Test-Driven Development (TDD)** is and explain why it is important for modern software and API development.

## ⏱️ Estimated Time
15 minutes total

## 🧠 Activity Structure
- **Part 1 (5-7 min):** Research core TDD concepts.
- **Part 2 (5 min):** Summarize what you learned.
- **Part 3 (3-5 min):** Share and discuss with a partner or small group.

## 📝 Instructions

### Part 1: Research (5-7 minutes)
Use the resources below to find answers to these questions:
- [ ] What is Test-Driven Development?
- [ ] What are the three phases of TDD (Red, Green, Refactor)?
- [ ] Why do teams use TDD in real projects?
- [ ] What are 2 benefits of TDD for API/backend work?
- [ ] What is one challenge or tradeoff of using TDD?

### Part 2: Quick Write-Up (5 minutes)
Create a short response (5-8 bullet points) that includes:
- A 1-2 sentence definition of TDD.
- The Red → Green → Refactor cycle in your own words.
- At least 2 reasons TDD is important.
- 1 example of how TDD could prevent bugs in an API.

### Part 3: Share (3-5 minutes)
Discuss your summary with a partner/group:
- [ ] Compare definitions of TDD.
- [ ] Compare benefits you found.
- [ ] Agree on one practical reason to use TDD in this course.

## ✅ Success Criteria
- [ ] You completed 5-7 minutes of focused research.
- [ ] You wrote a concise summary of TDD and its importance.
- [ ] You identified practical value for API development.

## 📚 Suggested Research Resources
- [Martin Fowler: Test-Driven Development](https://martinfowler.com/bliki/TestDrivenDevelopment.html)
- [Agile Alliance: Test Driven Development](https://www.agilealliance.org/glossary/tdd/)
- [Node.js Test Runner](https://nodejs.org/api/test.html)
- [Cypress Best Practices](https://docs.cypress.io/guides/references/best-practices)
- [TypeScript Handbook](https://www.typescriptlang.org/docs/)

## 🔍 Reflection Prompt
In one sentence: **How can TDD help you ship safer backend/API code?**
